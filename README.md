### Government Services & Compliance
- Standardized site templates tailored for ministries, agencies, and municipal departments
- Built-in publishing workflows for public notices, service directories, and permit portals
- Role-based approvals and audit logs to meet regulatory and transparency requirements
- Multi-language support for citizen-facing microsites
- Dedicated KRA service catalogues covering tax registration, filing, and compliance journeys
- Seamless integration points for iTax portals, PAYE processing, and digital tax stamps

### M-Pesa Integrations
- Native checkouts leveraging M-Pesa STK Push and QR flows
- Automated reconciliation of M-Pesa receipts inside custom dashboards
- Support for recurring billing with hybrid Stripe + M-Pesa payment plans
- Real-time notifications for successful payments, reversals, and chargebacks
